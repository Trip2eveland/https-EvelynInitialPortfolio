FONT TYPES


Fonts are a Treasure

Fonts were created for the purpose of expressing the level of importance of special events, life situations, etc. There are many fun fonts which are used for events such as weddings, birthdays, vacation, celebrations of all sorts. Below there are five (5) of the most standard fonts. First we have Serif. This font style is used mostly in formal business document, legal documents, and notices. Second is San Serif. San Serif is use for personal letters, emails, and casual communication. Third is Decorative. Through the use of creative decorative fonts, we can create dynamic and fabulous announcements, etc.. Fourth is Script. Script is one of my favorites. It can be fancy, intimate, sexy, and fun. Fifth is monospaced. Monospaced is a type writer and plain text font. Monospaced may have been the original font creation.
