WELCOME

Welcome to our humble site. Childcare comes in all shapes and sizes. We provide a home-like environment where children feel inclusive in the family unit. The atmosphere changes throughout the day as we transition to classroom curricula and free play. We do not claim to have all the answers; but we do our best to stay informed.
FINE MOTOR SKILLS DEVELOPMENT
Fine motor skills development is/are crucial to every child's development. Picking up the smallest items can help improve fine motor skills. Playing with play dough, kinetic sand, and water crafts can enhance sensory motor skills. We incorporate arts and crafts of many forms to allow growth and development in all areas. Procuring a creative and free thinking environment is supported through these activities.

The children also enjoy playground time (recess) to further develop their gross motor skills.

SELF ASSESSMENT TRAINING (CHILD DIRECTED)
We encourage children to explore their emotions. Through this they become independently capable of controlling and/or redirecting their behavior to a more positive and manageable level. Given the opportunity, children can self assess their own emotions. This is a major achievement which can be beneficial toward children learning to identify emotions of others around them (whether facial expressions or body language). I find this very useful in sensing stranger danger and/or recognizing when it may be unsafe to approach someone.



GROSS MOTOR SKILLS

Children are encouraged to run, jump, and climb to further develop their gross motor skills during playground time (recess). Kick ball, basketball, and catch are other activities offered to enhance their visual coordination as well.

LANGUAGE LITERACY

Singing, reading, and writing are simple ways to encourage children to build on language literacy. Here, we encourage independence so that every child can become more productive and confident throughout their development. Independent minded children have the potential to become the strong leaders of tomorrow.

PARTNERSHIP

Team playing is a necessity. Parents and facility employees must be able to openly communicate and keep one another apprised of the child's progress, at all times. Children do not fail! We as the adults fail the children by not attentively listening and/or by lack of knowledge. We must educate our children and display the behavior we want the children to exhibit. In other words, let's practice what we preach. Children are sponges. They absord everything they see and hear. Let us work together to closely monitor what our children are absorbing.

THANK YOU

We appreciate you visiting our site; and we hope you found it informative.
